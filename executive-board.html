Executive Board

We are a group of competitive individuals who have backgrounds in technology, finance, economics, marketing, and more.

Angela Dong

President

Angela is a Finance and Political Science double major aspiring for a career in asset management. She has previous experience researching international securities working at an equity research firm for the past two summer.


David Niezelski

VP, Operations

David is a finance and economics major at UMD. He was most recently a Fixed Income Data Analyst at Bloomberg and worked closely with the credit ratings team. Each week at CASH, he presents market news to club members.


George Lee

Treasurer

George is the treasurer for CASH. He also periodically presents lessons on financial management to club members. George is a finance major in the Robert H. Smith School of Business and is interested in both asset management and technology. He is also the founder of Dark Sonar, a cybersecurity company focused in fraud protection.


Andrew Lavenburg

Vice President

Andrew is a finance and economics major at UMD. He has professional experience working in banking, investing, and is the treasurer for CASH. He has a strong interest in capital markets and will be pursuing a career in the field. Next year, Andrew will be interning at Goldman Sachs in the Sales & Trading division.


Jamie Grossarth

VP, Marketing

Jamie is a finance and marketing major at the Robert H. Smith School of business and is most recently a finance intern at Social Tables, a Washington, D.C. based company.

UMD CASH

UMD CASH is the Community of Aspiring Shareholders Club based in the Robert H. Smith School of Business at the University of Maryland. Part of the Smith Undergraduate Student Association (SUSA), we aim to explore the world of finance in an eclectic manner.
