The Community of Aspiring Shareholders uses the dues it collects each semester to purchase food for the club, promote the club through campus advertising, and pay out cash prizes for the stock market challenge that members are encourage to participate in.

Dues can be paid in person to the CASH treasurer during club time or paid through Venmo through a Venmo account. Please note the following details when paying through Venmo:
Venmo Username: @umdcash
Phone #: 415-612-2882
Email: cash.terps@gmail.com

Using any of the above details should work. Please make sure that dues are paid to the correct account by verifying the above.

UMD CASH

UMD CASH is the Community of Aspiring Shareholders Club based in the Robert H. Smith School of Business at the University of Maryland. Part of the Smith Undergraduate Student Association (SUSA), we aim to educate our peers in finance through experiential means.
